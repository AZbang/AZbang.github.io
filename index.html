LearnWords
Small interactive service for learning foreign words.
At the moment there are 2000 commonly used words.
The base words were taken from the site http://4flaga.ru and later slightly tweaked and converted in the JSON format.

Over time, the game becomes more complicated, introducing new possibilities and combinations. I am sure that with each complete level you will be more and more proud of myself, because it's really hard!
